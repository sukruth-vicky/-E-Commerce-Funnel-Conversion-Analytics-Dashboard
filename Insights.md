# Funnel Flow Understanding

## E-Commerce Funnel Journey

1. User visits the website/app
2. User views products
3. User adds products to cart
4. User proceeds to checkout
5. User completes purchase

---

## Potential Drop-Off Areas

### Product Discovery Stage
Users may leave if:
- Products are irrelevant
- Search experience is poor
- UI is confusing

### Add to Cart Stage
Users may leave if:
- Prices are high
- Delivery charges appear expensive
- Product trust is low

### Checkout Stage
Users may leave if:
- Checkout process is complex
- Payment fails
- Too many steps exist

---

## Product Analytics Focus

The analysis will identify:
- Funnel leakage points
- Cart abandonment trends
- Conversion bottlenecks
- Revenue opportunities
- User behavior patterns
