# Product Strategy

## Objective

Improve customer conversion rates and reduce drop-offs across the e-commerce funnel.

---

## Funnel Optimization Strategies

### 1. Improve Product Discovery
- Personalized recommendations
- Better search functionality
- Trending product highlights

### 2. Reduce Cart Abandonment
- Simplify checkout process
- Offer limited-time discounts
- Improve payment reliability
- Enable guest checkout

### 3. Enhance Mobile Experience
- Optimize mobile UI/UX
- Reduce app loading times
- Simplify navigation

### 4. Improve Customer Retention
- Loyalty programs
- Personalized offers
- Email and notification reminders
- Repeat purchase incentives

### 5. Increase Conversion Rates
- Improve trust signals
- Simplify payment process
- Optimize checkout flow
- Reduce friction during purchase

---

## Expected Business Impact

- Increased revenue
- Improved customer retention
- Higher conversion rates
- Better customer experience
- Reduced customer drop-offs
