# Problem Statement

## Customer Problems

- Users abandon carts before completing purchases
- Complex checkout experiences reduce conversions
- Lack of personalized recommendations impacts engagement
- Slow or unclear purchase flow affects user experience

---

## Business Problems

- Low conversion rates
- Revenue leakage from abandoned carts
- Poor customer retention
- Inefficient customer acquisition ROI
- Loss of potential repeat customers

---

## Product Goal

The goal of this project is to analyze the e-commerce conversion funnel, identify customer drop-off points, improve conversion rates, optimize customer journeys, and increase revenue through data-driven product insights.
