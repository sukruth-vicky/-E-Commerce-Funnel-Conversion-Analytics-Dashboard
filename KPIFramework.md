# KPI Framework

## Funnel KPIs

### Website Visitors
Tracks total users entering the platform.

### Product Views
Measures product discovery and browsing engagement.

### Add to Cart Rate
Percentage of users adding products to their cart.

### Cart Abandonment Rate
Percentage of users leaving without completing purchase.

### Checkout Completion Rate
Measures successful checkout progression.

### Conversion Rate
Percentage of visitors completing purchases.

### Repeat Customer Rate
Measures customer retention and repeat purchasing behavior.

### Average Order Value (AOV)
Tracks average revenue generated per order.
